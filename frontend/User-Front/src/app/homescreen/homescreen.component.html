A B O U T



Purple Orchid is a shopping website, designed and developed as a project, keeping in mind about the case study provided to us by Virtusa. It is our Final Year Project, with a team of 5 members. Proper validations are exercised in order to avoid conflicts in the front-end. It has got two parts - Admin and User. In the Admin module, the admin is supposed to register himself, after that he is authenticated. Responsibilities of Admin is to edit product details including price, name, images and various other factors which are then reflected into the product database. He can also add various other products by providing the respective details. In the User module, the user registers himself at first, then he needs to log in into the website to view products. We also provide timely sale and coupons to make the website appear as smooth and real as possible. User can also make payments in the PayUMoney payments portal.
